How the platform works

From feedstock intake to monomer outputs, our model focuses on traceability, purity, and scalability.

1. Targeted depolymerization

PETase enzymes are applied under controlled conditions to break PET into terephthalic acid (TPA) and ethylene glycol (EG).

2. Purification workflow

Recovered monomers move through filtration and purification steps intended to support reuse in manufacturing-grade applications.

3. Circular re-entry

Purified outputs can re-enter supply chains as feedstock, lowering dependence on virgin fossil-derived inputs.
